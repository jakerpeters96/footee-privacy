How do I play?

Each day you get one hole and a yardage to the pin. Name footballers — their career goals are the yards your ball travels — and reach the pin in as few shots as you can. Full rules are in the app: tap the ? button on the main screen.

What are the “Easy” and “Hard” modes?

Easy concedes short putts and finishes the hole after 10 guesses. Hard requires you to hole out exactly. You can switch any time on the hole screen.

What’s a mulligan?

You can watch a short ad to scrap your last guess, up to twice a day. It’s optional — the game is fully playable without watching any ads.

How is my data handled?

Your progress is stored on your device. Footee shows ads via Google AdMob. Full details are in our Privacy Policy, where you can also learn how to manage your ad-consent choices.

How do I reset my stats?

Your streak and history live on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears them. Need something else? Just email us.
