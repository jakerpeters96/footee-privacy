Footee — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Footee mobile game (“Footee”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use it. We have built Footee to collect as little personal data as possible. By using the app you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who we are

Footee is operated by the developer of Footee (“the developer”). For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at jakerpeters96@gmail.com.

2. Information stored on your device

Your game progress — including your current streak, puzzle history, today’s result and difficulty settings — is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload this information to our servers, and it is removed if you delete the app.

3. Information used to run the game

  • Daily puzzle content. The app requests the current daily puzzle from our hosting provider (Supabase). This is a read-only request for game content and does not send any personal information about you, and it does not require an account or login.
  • Local reminders. If you grant notification permission, the app schedules a daily reminder that is generated entirely on your device. No push tokens or personal data are collected or transmitted for this feature.

4. Advertising

Footee displays advertising provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process information such as your device’s advertising identifier, device and diagnostic information, IP address, and general app usage. Depending on the consent you provide, ads may be personalized (based on this information) or non‑personalized (contextual only).

You can learn how Google uses this data here: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

5. Your choices and consent (EEA & UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, the app will ask for your consent for advertising data processing the first time you play, using Google’s consent mechanism. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time:

  • Re-open the privacy / consent options inside the app to update your selection; and
  • Reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

The legal bases we rely on are your consent (for personalized advertising) and our legitimate interests in operating and improving the game. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict processing, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in the UK, the Information Commissioner’s Office).

6. Children

Footee is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with the service providers needed to run the app — our content host (Supabase) and our advertising provider (Google AdMob) — each acting under their own privacy terms.

8. Data retention and security

On-device data remains until you clear it or uninstall the app. Advertising data is retained by Google in accordance with its policies. We use reasonable technical measures to protect information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries outside your own, including the United States. Where required, such transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions or requests: jakerpeters96@gmail.com.
